Guidance
Kaltura integration with Zoom allows you to automatically upload your Zoom cloud recordings to Kaltura after your meeting ends.
Prerequisites
  • A Paid Zoom Account
  • Access to Zoom Cloud Recording / Your Zoom account must have permission to access recordings.
  • Kaltura Publisher Administrator Account.
Instructions
  1. Login to the Zoom MarketPlace.
  2. Discover Kaltura Connector.
  3. Now click on Authorize.
  4. Authorize the following scopes the Kaltura Connector uses.
    • View and manage your user information
    • View your user information
    • View your recordings
  5. Now you will be redirected to the Kaltura configuration Page.

Kaltura Integration

If you do not have zoom recording permissions, you must use your Zoom email Id as your folder ID.
